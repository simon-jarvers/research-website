About

Hi, I believe that making AI safe and beneficial for all is the most important problem of our time.

As with many big problems, probably no single strategy will be enough. To avoid catastrophic risks, we need people working across AI governance, AI alignment, and field-building for AI safety simultaneously. My own focus is AI governance: how do the rules and frameworks meant to make AI safe actually play out inside the organisations building it?

Outside of research, I love to spend time in nature, build my own furniture, and try to beat my personal records as a hobby powerlifter.
