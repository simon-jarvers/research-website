What does AI Governance mean in practice?

About me →

Hi there, I am an Action Researcher at TUM working at Augmented Industries as an AI Governance Specialist. I want to learn how AI regulation, like the EU AI Act, translates into the daily practice of building AI systems. My research focuses on the gap between formal compliance and what actually happens inside development teams.
